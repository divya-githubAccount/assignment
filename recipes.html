Recipes - World's Best Scrambled Eggs

I adopted this recipe from a book called Sydney food by Bit Granger.Ever since tasting these on my first visit to restaurant in Kings Cross.
Sydney. I have been after the recipe. I have since transformed it into what I really believe.

Ingredients

The following ingredients make one serving:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of butter(10g)
  • A pinch of salt
  • Freshly miled black pepper
  • 3 fresh chives(chopped)

Instructions

  1. Whisk eggs,cream, and salt in a bowl
  2. Melt the butter in a stick pan over a high heat(taking care not burn the butter).
  3. Pour eggs mixture into a pan and wait util it starts setting around the edge of pan(around 20 seconds)
  4. Using a wooden spatuls, bring the mixture into the center as if were an omelet, and let it cook for another 20 seconds
  5. Fold the content in again, leave for 20 seconds, and repeat until eggs are only just done
  6. Grind a light sprinkling of freshly miled pepper over eggs and blend in some chopped fresh chives
